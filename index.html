One difficult street crossing in the Lloyd District, Portland, Oregon.

Holladay Park Plaza is a continuing care retirement community. NE 16th Avenue and parking lots separate Holladay Park Plaza residents from direct access to Lloyd Center.

Lloyd Center has- covered walking areas, an indoor ice rink, a food court, a restuarant, movie theaters, department stores, including Sears, Macy's and Nordstroms.

The shortest route from Holladay Park Plaza's main entrance is west, across NE 16th Ave, through a parking lot, and into Sears. To take this route is to jaywalk and to scramble the few feet up and over a planted median strip. The route is popular and the legal alternatives seem inconvenient.
